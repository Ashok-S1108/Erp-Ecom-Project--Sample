📌 Controllers List
1. User & Authentication
user.controller.js – handles registration, login, profile, update user, delete user, change password, etc.

auth.controller.js (optional) – if you want a separate file for token refresh & authentication-only logic.

2. Product Management
product.controller.js – CRUD for products, search/filter, stock updates (integrated with ERP inventory).

category.controller.js – CRUD for product categories/subcategories.

inventory.controller.js – manages stock, adjustments, and low-stock alerts.

3. Orders & Payments
order.controller.js – create orders, update status, track delivery.

payment.controller.js – handles payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, etc.), refunds.

invoice.controller.js – generate and manage invoices.

4. ERP – Procurement & Suppliers
supplier.controller.js – CRUD for suppliers.

purchaseOrder.controller.js – create and manage purchase orders for suppliers.

inventoryTransaction.controller.js – logs all stock inflows/outflows.

5. CRM – Customers
customer.controller.js – manages customer records (could reuse user controller if customers are User role).

review.controller.js – product reviews & ratings.

6. Notifications & Support
notification.controller.js – send and manage in-app/email notifications.

feedback.controller.js – handle customer feedback or support tickets.

7. Reports & Analytics
report.controller.js – sales reports, inventory reports, customer activity reports.

8. Admin
admin.controller.js – dashboard stats, system settings, role management.
